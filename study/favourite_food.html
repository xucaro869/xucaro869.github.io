Tomato Beef Tail Stew

Photo by 漂亮马婆

Ingredients:

  • Tomatoes (however many depends on how thick you want the stew!)
  • Segments of chopped up beef tail (you can get it at many stores, like Foodland Supermarket)
  • Salt, sugar and 2 slices of ginger
  • Water
  • Any other vegetables, if desired.

Directions:

  1. Leave the beef tail chunks until they are thawed out.
  2. Boil them and remove the bubbles from the pot. Pour out all the water.
  3. Put the chunks into a high pressure cooker along with 2 slices of onion, add water, and cook for 30 mins. to 1 hour.
  4. Cool it on the countertop, and then in the fridge or outside, if it has been snowing. Leave overnight.
  5. Remove the top layer of white fat using a spoon. Put the rest into a pot.
  6. Slice tomatoes and put them into the pot and heat until the jello-like substance around the beef tail chunks melts.
  7. Add 2 tbsp. of sugar and boil until soup becomes red with this color - TOMATOES.
  8. Add a pinch of salt to the mixture.
  9. Add any other vegetables within 5 minutes of finishing your stew.
  10. Pour it all into a big bowl and bring it to the dinner table.
And now, your tomato beef tail stew is ready to eat!
